About This Project

This site was created to encourage daily self-care and emotional check-ins. 🌺

🌿 My Top Self-Care Activities

  • Listening to music
  • Bubble baths
  • Journaling
  • Streching or Yoga
  • Lighting a candle and chilling

😴 Steps for a Calm Night

  1. Put your phone away
  2. Make a cup of tea
  3. Write down 3 things that make you happy
  4. Do 5 minutes of deep breathing
  5. Rest and recharge

Self-Care Q + A

What's one quick self-care tip?

Take three slow breaths. That's it. You're already doing great.

How can I unwind at night?

Dim the lights, light a candle, and play calming sounds deom your favorite playlist.

What if I feel overwhelmed?

Pause. Write down three things you're grateful for. Shift your energy gently.
